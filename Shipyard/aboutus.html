About Us

Tamilnadu Shipyard Limited is a leading shipbuilding and repair company with a rich history of excellence and innovation. Established in 1985, we have been at the forefront of the maritime industry, delivering high-quality vessels and services to clients worldwide. Our state-of-the-art facilities and highly skilled workforce enable us to meet the diverse needs of our clients, from commercial shipping to defense and offshore industries.

We are committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility, ensuring that our operations have minimal impact on the environment. Our mission is to provide innovative and reliable maritime solutions that exceed our clients' expectations while contributing to the growth of the global maritime industry.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class shipbuilding and repair services through innovation, quality, and sustainability, ensuring customer satisfaction and contributing to the growth of the maritime industry.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in the maritime industry, recognized for our excellence, innovation, and commitment to environmental stewardship.
